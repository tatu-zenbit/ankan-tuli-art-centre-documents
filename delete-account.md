# Account and Data Deletion
**Ankan Tuli Art Center**

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and remove your personal data from our systems, please follow the steps below.

### üìù How to Request Deletion
To initiate the deletion of your account, you can use either of the following methods:

1. **Via Email (Recommended):** Send an email to **tatu.zenbit@gmail.com** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the email address used to register the account.
   
2. **In-App:** You may contact the administrator (Teacher) directly to remove your student records and login access.

### üõë What Data Will Be Deleted?
Once your request is processed, the following information will be permanently removed from our Firebase database:
* Your Name, Email, and Phone Number.
* All linked Student Records (Roll numbers, Blood Group, etc.).
* Your Payment History and Transaction IDs (UTRs).

Requests are typically processed within **7 business days**. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
